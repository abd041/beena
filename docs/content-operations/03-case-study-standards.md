# Case Study Governance

## Purpose

Portfolio entries represent **credible consulting engagements** — not marketing fiction. Readers should infer how BEEÑA-E operates with biopharma and emerging biotech sponsors.

> **Note:** Case studies currently use static data in `src/lib/data/case-studies.ts` with optional Supabase sync. Until a portfolio CMS exists, follow this standard for all new entries in code or database.

## Required narrative blocks

1. **Title** — Specific modality or problem (e.g. intravitreal biologic, gene therapy portfolio)
2. **Client label** — Anonymized tier acceptable (“Top-20 biopharma sponsor”)
3. **Summary** — 1–2 sentences on scope and outcome
4. **Challenge** — Constraints: timeline, governance, agency uncertainty, resourcing, competitive entry
5. **Approach** — What BEEÑA-E did (workshops, gap analysis, briefing packages, governance cadence)
6. **Results** — 4–5 bullets; outcomes not adjectives
7. **Metrics** — Few, believable labels (markets, interactions, assets — not “10x ROI”)
8. **Service slugs** — Link to `/services/[slug]`
9. **Related insight** (optional) — `relatedInsightSlug` for cross-link

## Realism checklist

- [ ] Timelines match modality (gene therapy ≠ 3-month launch prep)
- [ ] Regulatory steps named where relevant (pre-IND, Scientific Advice, HTA)
- [ ] Stakeholders implied (medical, access, CMC, board)
- [ ] No guaranteed outcomes or superlatives
- [ ] Metrics are countable and modest
- [ ] Challenge acknowledges what was **not** known at start

## Language

Use:

- “Board-approved prioritization”
- “Aligned CMC and clinical narratives”
- “Formal agency interactions”

Avoid:

- “Revolutionized their pipeline”
- “Guaranteed approval”
- “Industry-leading results”

## Commercialization cases

Include: launch governance, medical narrative, access sequencing, regional variance.

## Regulatory cases

Include: agency type, meeting outcomes, endpoint framework — without claiming confidential agency quotes unless approved.

## Review

Portfolio additions require **senior advisor sign-off** before publish.
