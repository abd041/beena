# Brand Language System

Code reference: `src/lib/content-governance/brand-language.ts` (banned/caution phrases enforced in CMS on publish).

## Voice pillars

1. **Institutional** — We advise; we do not sell hype.
2. **Ophthalmic-specific** — Retina, cornea, gene therapy, intravitreal, imaging.
3. **Operationally credible** — Governance, agencies, endpoints, payers, timelines.
4. **Globally professional** — US, EU, and selected regions; no parochial slang.

## Preferred phrasing

### Consultations

- Schedule a Consultation
- Discuss strategic priorities
- Confidential consultation
- Contact Our Team

### Regulatory strategy

- regulatory pathway · agency interaction · Scientific Advice · pre-IND  
- endpoint acceptability · ophthalmic precedent · briefing package

### Clinical development

- integrated development plan · clinical program · trial architecture  
- imaging charter · evidence generation

### Market access

- evidence plan · HTA · payer engagement · budget impact (when substantiated)

### Commercialization

- launch readiness · medical affairs alignment · lifecycle planning · KOL engagement

### Ophthalmic innovation (use carefully)

- advanced modalities · gene therapy · sustained-delivery platform  
- intravitreal biologic · ocular surface disease

## Words and phrases to avoid (*blocked in CMS*)

game-changing · disruptive · revolutionary · world-class · best-in-class · cutting-edge · synergy · move the needle · paradigm shift · unlock growth · guaranteed results · elite biotech · thought leader

## Use with caution (*warnings in CMS*)

accelerate · transformative · innovation pipeline · unprecedented · seamless · holistic · turnkey

**Replacement guidance:**

| Weak | Stronger |
|------|----------|
| accelerate timelines | support a defined path to Phase 2 |
| transformative | advanced / first-in-class (if factual) |
| innovation pipeline | development portfolio |
| holistic approach | integrated advisory across functions |

## Consistency

- **BEEÑA-E Consulting** on first reference; BEEÑA-E acceptable thereafter
- **Ophthalmic** not “eye care” in B2B content (RISE exception)
- **FDA / EMA** capitalized; do not say “the FDA” in every sentence

## AI-generated content policy

- AI may assist research outlines **only**
- Human author owns accuracy, tone, and compliance
- Never publish unedited AI output
