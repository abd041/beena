# Article Content Framework

## Goal

Every Insight should answer: **What should an ophthalmic development or commercial leader do differently after reading this?**

## Standard structure

Use the CMS **Insert article template** or copy from `src/lib/content-governance/article-template.ts`.

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Executive summary** | 2–3 sentences: context, stakes, recommended direction |
| **Strategic context** | Market, modality, capital, or competitive dynamics |
| **Regulatory / scientific implications** | Agencies, endpoints, imaging, biomarkers, modalities |
| **Commercial / access considerations** | Payers, HTA, launch — when relevant |
| **Operational challenges** | CMC, CRO, cross-functional, regional harmonization |
| **Practical recommendations** | Actionable guidance (numbered or short blocks) |
| **Key takeaways** | 3 bullets maximum — specific, not generic |
| **Related advisory support** | One neutral line tying to a service area |

## Depth requirements (*enforced on publish*)

| Metric | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Word count | ≥ 900 words |
| Section headings | ≥ 4 lines starting with `## ` |
| Read time | Typically 8–14 minutes |
| Cover image | Required |
| Related service | Strongly recommended (CMS field) |

## Formatting

- Separate paragraphs with a **blank line**
- No HTML in body (plain text + `##` headings only)
- Avoid ALL CAPS, excessive bold, or emoji
- Use en-dashes for ranges (e.g. 120–160 characters)

## Title guidelines

- Specific and executive-facing  
  **Good:** “Global Regulatory Pathways for Novel Intravitreal Biologics”  
  **Weak:** “The Future of Eye Care Innovation”

## Excerpt guidelines

- 40–160 characters
- State topic + audience benefit without hype
- Works as standalone summary in listings and social previews

## Internal linking

- Set **Related service** in CMS
- Optionally reference a portfolio slug in prose when a public case exists
- End with consultation path via site CTA (not embedded sales copy in body)

## What to avoid

- Trend pieces without sponsor implications
- Lists of “top 10 trends” without analysis
- Content that could apply to any therapeutic area unchanged
- Unedited AI drafts (always human review for accuracy and tone)
